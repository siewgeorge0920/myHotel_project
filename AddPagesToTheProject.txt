How to add your pages in to this project:

Step1: create your pages(.jsx) inside your folder(client/src/pages/George || Lincoln)
Remember: you don't have to have header and footer in your pages as they are already fixed in the App.jsx.

Step2: after your page is created, going to App.jsx file(line 15---30): import your page. (comments and structure is already there)
// Import Derrick's new pages
// import Sauna from './pages/Derrick/Sauna';

step3: going down from App.jsx to <Router>/<Routes> inside <Routes>(line100---110) add route path of your page (comments and structure is already there)
// Derrick: 
//  <Route path="/spa/sauna" element={<Sauna />} />


step4: going to client/src/components/ Header.jsx. 
 Fix the dropdown list links by asking your personal Ai assistant.

//EXPERIENCE DROPDOWN 
// Lincoln 

// SPA & WELLNESS DROPDOWN
// Derrick

//INCLUSIVE RESORT DROPDOWN
//George

this is much easier to manage.
